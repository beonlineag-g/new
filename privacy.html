Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-22

Overview

MemoryMatch Pro is a casual web game. We do not require account signup and we minimize data collection.

Information We Handle

  • Local data: high scores and game preferences are stored in your browser’s localStorage. This data is never sent to our servers.
  • Contact messages: if you choose to contact us via our form, we receive the information you submit to respond to your inquiry.

Cookies

We do not use cookies. The entire game experience is self-contained in your browser.

Your Choices

  • You can clear your browser's site data at any time to remove locally stored scores.
  • Using your browser's private or incognito mode will prevent any data from being stored.

Contact

Questions? Use the contact form on the Home page.

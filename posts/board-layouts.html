Designing Fair Difficulty Curves

Keep players hooked with challenge that teaches, not punishes.

Published 2025-08-22

Good difficulty invites mastery instead of frustration, guiding players to improve with every run.

Start generous

Let newcomers earn quick wins and feel empowered before raising the stakes.

Ramp gently

Scale enemy speed, spawn rates, or puzzle complexity in small, steady steps.

Telegraph danger

Show clear cues—animations, sounds, or patterns—so success comes from learning rather than guessing.
